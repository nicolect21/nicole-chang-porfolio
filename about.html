Bio

Nicole Chang is a Venezuelan-Chinese American designer, musician, and video editor based in Dallas. Chang is currently an undergrad student at the University of Texas at Dallas pursuing a Media Arts and Design degree. Chang uses a mix of digital and physical tools to realize her creative projects. With inspiration coming from a variety of things such as medieval revival, early 2000's technology, and all things fantasy, Chang adds her own personal touch to any project. Chang is always looking for opportunities to try new and exciting endevours that challenge her pre-existing skillset.
